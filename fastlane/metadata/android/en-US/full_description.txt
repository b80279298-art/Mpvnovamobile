mpvNova is a TV-first video player for Android TV, Google TV, and Fire OS based on <a href="https://github.com/mpv-player/mpv">libmpv</a>.

Features:
* Hardware and software video decoding
* libass support for styled subtitles
* Secondary (or dual) subtitle support
* Advanced video settings (interpolation, debanding, scalers, ...)
* Play network streams with the "Open URL" function
* Background playback, Picture-in-Picture, remote and keyboard input supported
